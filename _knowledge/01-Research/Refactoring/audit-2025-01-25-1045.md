# Technical Debt & Refactoring Audit - January 25, 2025

## Executive Summary

The TPN Dynamic Text Editor codebase shows significant technical debt accumulation from rapid prototyping and feature expansion. **Critical issues identified**: App.svelte has grown to 2,477 lines (5x the recommended 300-500 line limit), extensive code duplication across service layers, incomplete Svelte 5 rune migration, and architectural inconsistencies that impact development velocity.

**Priority Focus**: Immediate attention needed on component decomposition and service layer consolidation to prevent further technical debt accumulation.
